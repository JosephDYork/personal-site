I care a lot about long-term craft: writing code that survives, research that compounds, and habits that hold up under pressure.

The shared thread is patience. Most meaningful technical progress comes from steady iteration rather than one dramatic breakthrough.

I try to optimize for clarity first, then speed. Fast systems are great, but understandable systems are what let teams keep shipping.
